About Youth Commission Mylapore Madras

Youth Commission Chennai is the umbrella youth organization of the Archdiocese of Madras-Mylapore consisting of movements: CCYM and YCS/YSM, which strive to build a new society, God's kingdom based on the values of love, peace, truth, equality & justice.
